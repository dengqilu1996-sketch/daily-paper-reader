## 摘要
研究假设短跑运动会引起脂肪组织（AT）中与脂解相关的基因表达变化，且生长激素（GH）作为刺激因素发挥作用。十二名健康男女进行了3次30秒全力自行车冲刺（SIT）和六次无负荷骑行（CON）。运动前和运动后2小时进行AT活检。SIT组的血清GH反应高于CON组，但在AT中未发现与脂解相关的差异表达基因（DEGs）富集。然而，GH受体是AT中少数在SIT与CON间存在交互作用的DEGs之一，且预选的GH相关靶标CISH、PTEN和G0S2的表达变化与运动引起的GH增加相关。这支持SIT通过GH介导影响脂解的效应。在全基因组分析中，ARHGAP24、TAF4B、ARID5B、MIR604和MIR938的表达变化与GH增加相关。这些基因的功能尚不完全清楚，但先前已证明它们与AT有一定关联。性别影响运动引起的GH反应和GH反应基因CISH的表达。下调最显著的基因包括核心时钟基因PER1、NR1D1和CIART，它们在SIT和CON中均下降。这强调了在今后研究AT脂解调节的运动实验中，控制昼夜变化和性别的必要性。

## Abstract
It was hypothesised that sprint exercise induces changes in adipose tissue (AT) gene expression related to lipolysis, and that growth hormone (GH) acts as a stimulus. Twelve healthy males and females perform 3x30-s all-out cycle sprints (SIT) and six unloaded cycling (CON). AT biopsies were performed pre and 2 hours post-exercise. Serum GH response was greater in SIT than CON, but no enrichment of differentially expressed genes (DEGs) related to lipolysis was found in AT. However, the GH-receptor was one of few DEGs in AT with interaction between SIT and CON and exercise-induced changes in expression of the pre-selected GH-related targets, CISH, PTEN and G0S2 were associated with exercise-induced increase in GH. This supports a GH-mediated effect of SIT on lipolysis. In a genome-wide analysis, exercise-induced changes in expression of ARHGAP24, TAF4B, ARID5B, MIR604 and MIR938, were associated with increase in GH. The function of these genes is not well understood, but some relationship to AT has earlier been demonstrated Sex influenced GH-response to exercise and expression of the GH-responsive gene CISH. Among the most downregulated genes were the core clock genes PER1, NR1D1 and CIART, which decreased in both SIT and CON. This underscores the necessity to control for diurnal variations and sex in future exercise studies on regulation of AT lipolysis.

### 7. 优点
- **设计严谨的时间对照**：运动后2小时活检结合无负荷对照，有效区分运动本身效应与非特异性昼夜波动。
- **多层级分析策略**：从经典通路、预设靶标到全基因组扫描层层递进，减少假阳性并增加发现的可靠性。
- **生理相关性高**：采用人体在体活检和自然运动模式（全力冲刺），比体外或动物模型更贴近真实运动场景。
- **突出方法论警示**：明确指出昼夜节律和性别混杂的重要性，为领域内研究设计提供直接改进建议。

- **应用限制**：结论适用于健康青年群体，在代谢性疾病或不同训练状态人群中的可迁移性未知。
